    I'm Taylor.

    I'm from Louisiana, currently in San Francisco. I have a Master's degree in chemistry and have been in the refining/chemical industry for about 7 years. After a few role changes, I slowly began transitioning into tech, until I realized how much I enjoyed database management, problem-solving, and the continuous learning that comes with programming. I was also lucky enough to experience DefCon in 2022. It opened my eyes! I knew I wanted to make the change completely! I started coding at the beginning of 2022 and haven't stopped. I recently graduated from a fullstack software engineering boot camp! I enjoy working on the backend and learning how technologies communicate with each other, working to optimize speed and space, and keeping everything secure!
